sun, surf, shop

Come visit the beautiful Sunshine Coast and enjoy a day of fun in the sun. Soak in the rays on the beach, have a shop at the local boutiques, or check out where the locals dine for some of the best food on the East Coast. It's not all about the beach though, check out the lush hinterlands or take a stroll thorugh the National Park.

Enjoy some of the best food the East Coast has to offer, with award winning restaurants to quirky little cafes there something for everyones palet.

Noosa is a world-famous surfing destination, known for its expansive beaches and colourful bays. Sit in the sand and soak in the sun or try your line with some beach fishing. Looking for some water filled adverntures? Canoeing, kayaking paddleboarding are ideal activities on the Noosa river. Hire a boat and wet a line or just cruise around and enjoy the views.

Noosa is a shopaholics paradise with many precincts each offering a range of different boutiques with items to suit everyone. If you’re looking for fresh produce or something a little more original try the weekend markets.
