Autumn Bliss

Come visit beautiful bright, picturesque in every season, especially in Autumn where the tree turn auburn and the golden leaves scatter the ground. There's something for everyone including bushwalks, hikes, biking, aquatic adventures and climbing for the more adventureous, and wine tours, brewery tours and markets for the ones wanting a more relaxed journey.

Enjoy what that local producers have to offer fresh off the farm to the plate. Bright had many good cafes and restaurants on offer as well as a winery and brewery tours and craft chocolate.

Bright offers the idea climate for outdoor activities such as hiking and bushwalking. Make your way hrough rivers and valleys and stop at a mountaion top lookout to take in what the countryside has to offer. Looking for some water filled adverntures? Canoeing, kayaking and raftings are ideal for The Ovens and Buckland rivers. Swimming and boating are also avaliable. See what the locals can make, bake and grow at the Birght Markets. Wether it's a handmade gift, artwork, or food and wine, you'll find it at the marketplace.
