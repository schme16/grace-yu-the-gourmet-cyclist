The garden city

Toowoomba is a picturesque mountain city located in South East Queensland. It offers a relaxed atmosphere and carefree urban living without the hassle and bustle of the big city. Toowoomba has definitely earned it name as the garden city. With thick tree lined streets and an extensive range of parks and gardens scattered throughout the rural city, there is no shortage of what nature has to offer.

Sit down and enjoy some coffee and cake and one of the many cafes Toowoomba has to offer. Relax and rewind and take in the views and enjoy the good food.

Toowoomba has a range of different galleries for the more creative adventurers. Stop by the regional art gallery, the USQ gallery or the Grid, for the more contemporary, or just have a walk around town and check out the wall art.

It is the garden city after all and the local gardens are a must. With a range of different gardens scattered around the city, there is something to suit everyone's spring adventurer.

 Stop by the Empire theare and see what's on, or stop by the USQ concert theatre and see what shows they have in store. There is never a shortage of theatrical and musical acts to see in Toowoomba.
